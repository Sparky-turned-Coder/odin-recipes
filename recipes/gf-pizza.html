Gluten Free Homemade Pizza

A gluten free pizza using cauliflower crust and all gluten free ingredients. Easy to make, pop into an oven, and eat within twenty minutes.
As long as the ingredients are listed as 'Gluten Free', you can throw whatever you want on there for a quick, healthy pizza night!

Ingredients

  • cauliflower pizza dough
  • pizza sauce
  • vegetable oil
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • garlic seasoning
  • onion powder
  • turkey pepperonis
  • green peppers (optional)

Steps:

  1. Knead and flatten cauliflower dough to desired size and thickness of a circle
  2. Spray a light amount of oil and lather over entire surface of pizza crust
  3. Sprinkle garlic seasoning and onion powder across oil
  4. Add desired amount of pizza sauce, lather equally across the crust
  5. Add desired amount of shredded cheese across the crust
  6. Add desired amount of pepperonis and green peppers
  7. Place in oven at 420 degrees for 21 minutes(or until crust looks however you like it).
  8. Remove and let sit for a few minutes.
  9. Slice, dice and serve it up.
