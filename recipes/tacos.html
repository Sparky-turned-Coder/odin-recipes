Tacos

Delicous taco night. Self explanatory. Can you tell you that I'm not much of a cook? :)

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • hard shells and corn tortillas
  • cup of water
  • shredded cheese
  • lettuce
  • sour cream
  • mexican style rice
  • refined beans

Steps:

  1. Brown ground beef in a sizeable skillet on the stove
  2. When cooked, add seasoning and a cup of water and stir until water is mostly gone
  3. heat taco shells in oven for 5 minutes on 350 degrees
  4. microwave mexican rice for 2 1/2 minutes. Stir violently.
  5. microwave refined beans for 3 1/2 minutes. Stir violently.
  6. wrap corn tortillas in paper towel and heat in microwave for 1 minute
  7. apply desired amount of taco meat in shells and tortillas
  8. apply lettuce
  9. apply rice and beans
  10. Enjoy!
