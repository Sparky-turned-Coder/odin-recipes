Dark Chocolate Oats Cereal

A simple way to add some pizazz to our favorite cereal Honey Bunches of Oats!

Ingredients

  • Honey Bunches of Oats cereal
  • Vanilla flavored almond milk
  • Dark chocolate granola

Steps:

  1. Pour desired amount of cereal into your favorite bowl
  2. sprinkle a cup of Dark chocolate granola on top
  3. Add desired amount of Vanilla almond milk
  4. Find a spoon and enjoy!
